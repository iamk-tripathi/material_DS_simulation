# App Bar

App Bar anchors screen title, navigation affordances, and top-level actions within a consistent surface. It helps users understand where they are, how to go back, and what high-priority actions are available.

## Do's and Don'ts

<DosDonts
  dos={[
    'Use app bar to frame the screen title and top-level actions',
    'Show a back arrow only when there is a real previous step',
    'Keep trailing actions secondary to the title',
    'Use bottom app bar only when persistent shell actions are needed',
    'Match app bar size to the screen hierarchy and content density',
  ]}
  donts={[
    'Overload the app bar with too many competing actions',
    'Replace a clear title with only icons or avatar',
    'Show back navigation when the screen is already top-level',
    'Use bottom app bar for content-specific actions that belong inside the body',
    'Rely on color alone to communicate hierarchy or affordance',
  ]}
/>
