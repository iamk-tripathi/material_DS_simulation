# Sheet

Sheet presents supplementary content anchored to the bottom or side edge of the interface. It solves the need to show supporting content in a dedicated surface without leaving the current flow.

## Do's and Don'ts

<DosDonts
  dos={[
    'Use sheets for supplementary content anchored to an edge',
    'Choose bottom or side placement based on the content flow',
    'Use modal sheets when the content should temporarily take focus',
    'Keep the sheet title and content concise',
    'Use the back affordance only when the sheet flow requires it',
  ]}
  donts={[
    'Use sheets for unrelated content with no connection to the current flow',
    'Treat every sheet as modal by default',
    'Overload a sheet with dense, unrelated sections',
    'Show a back button when there is no back action',
    'Rely on sheet placement alone to communicate importance',
  ]}
/>
